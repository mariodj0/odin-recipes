Description

Waakye is a Ghanaian dish of cooked rice and beans. It is cooked in such a way that the rice and beans take on a reddish brown colour. It is served with a tomato-based stew and can be eaten with a variety of side dishes including garri (shito), spaghetti, salad, stewed gizzard, fried fish or boiled eggs. Waakye is a popular Ghanaian food which is enjoyed

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 cup of black eyed peas
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and beans together and soak in water for about 30 minutes.
  2. Drain the water and rinse the rice and beans.
  3. Place the rice and beans in a pot and add enough water to cover the rice and beans.
  4. Add the baking soda and salt and stir.
