Description

Boiled yam is a Ghanaian dish of cooked sliced yam. It is cooked Till it has a soft consistency such that a fork can easily be driven through it. It is usually served with a tomato-based stew and can be eaten with a variety of side dishes including shito,salad, stewed gizzard, fried fish or boiled eggs. Ingredients

  • 2 cups of yam
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Wash the yam and peel the skin off.
  2. Cut the yam into slices.
  3. Place the yam in a pot and add enough water to cover the yam.
  4. Add the salt and stir.
  5. Place the pot on a stove and cook for 30 minutes.
  6. Remove the yam from the pot and serve.
